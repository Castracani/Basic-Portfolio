About Me

I've been professionally tested on my bio writing capabilites, and I've scored five standard deviations above the mean according to internationally recoginized standards. I'm basically a celebrity in my hometown; in fact the town council unanimously voted to erect a statue of me just last month (send me your fanmail here!).


I love going out and stretching myself to bask in the Florida sun, and I also love going for a quick dip in the water and swim lazily around until something catches my attention.
